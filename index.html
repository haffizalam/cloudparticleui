V.C ROY

(Managing Director (VMSPL))

A marketing strategist and broadband applications & distribution expert, Mr V C Roy has more than 20 years of experience. He was Consultant & Advisor to Telecom Regulatory Authority of India, and has been a Government Spokesperson on Conditional Access Systems. He launched the market of DTH and the services of converged medium for the first time and worked towards the future of DTH, Triple play, IPTV with the huge market potential of India. He headed WBCL (World Broadband Communications Ltd.) as the CEO for 15 years. He was special advisor to the Internet exchange in launching the domain “.in” for India. He lead from front India’s first digital channel at Bangalore and was also Project co-coordinator for Motorola in 2004-05.
